About the Firm

CA Prakash M Oswal & Co. is a full-service chartered accountancy firm committed to delivering excellence in audit, taxation, accounting, and advisory services. Founded with a vision to provide holistic financial solutions, our practice serves a diverse clientele ranging from startups and SMEs to established corporates across various industries.

With years of experience and a team of dedicated professionals, we pride ourselves on understanding our clients' unique challenges and delivering tailored solutions that drive growth, ensure compliance, and enhance operational efficiency.

Our approach is rooted in integrity, technical expertise, and a commitment to building long-term relationships based on trust and results. Whether you need statutory compliance support, strategic financial guidance, or comprehensive business advisory, we are your reliable partner in navigating the complexities of the financial and regulatory landscape.
