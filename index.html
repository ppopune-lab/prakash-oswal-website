Practice overview

CA Prakash M Oswal & Co. is a full-service chartered accountancy firm committed to delivering excellence in audit, taxation, accounting, and advisory services. The firm serves a diverse clientele ranging from startups and SMEs to established corporates across industries.

Our approach is grounded in integrity, technical depth, and a practical understanding of how businesses work on the ground, ensuring recommendations are implementable and outcome-focused.

Whether you need statutory compliance support, internal audits, tax planning, or strategic financial guidance, we work as an extended finance partner to your organisation.
